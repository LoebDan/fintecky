CARPOGO

 About CarPoGo

CarPoGo is a platform designed to bring potential ride-sharers together. CarPoGo enables its users to come into contact with CarPoGo approved car drivers for ride sharing opportunities. This pairing of a passenger with a driver is done in an effective and time saving manner. Unlike other ride sharing platforms, CarPoGo utilises a Hub System. Through using centralised Hubs, both the driver and the passenger will know where the start and end location is situated.

CarPoGo Benefits

CarPoGo creates ride sharing opportunities where you can:
◆ Make new friends.
◆ Help save the environment.
◆ Save money.
It is proven that ride sharing relieves stress from passengers having to find parking, and deal with traffic hassles. Ride sharing can significantly decrease the amount of cars on the road at a time. With ride sharing, you can also help create a cleaner environment. An added benefit is that anyone can become a CarPoGo driver, with the necessary requirements of course, and make a little extra money while going about your normal daily routine. CarPoGo uses a cashless system, where the user can topup their account instantaneously. Once the ride has ended, the transaction will occur automatically, something you do not have to stress about. A driver receives their payment through the credit card system, with the opportunity to withdraw the credits, or use them to pay for rides.

Be a Passenger

Being a CarPoGo passenger is easy. As a passenger, you can either schedule a ride for later in the day or week, or you can book an immediate ride. Drivers will see your ride posts and offer to give you a lift. As a passenger, you can also view driver scheduled rides and ask to join their rides. Since CarPoGo utilises a Hub system, with central Hubs, the pairing of a driver and passenger is more likely to occur. Therefore, you can have ease of mind, knowing you have a high chance of being paired with a driver. Unlike other ride sharing platforms, CarPoGo has a Party System. A party leader initiates a party and shares a unique code with potential members. Once everyone has joined, the leader can book a ride for the entire party. This ensures you can ride share with your friends or family. With CarPoGo, the more people there are in the car during a driver's trip, the less the fare will be for the passengers and the more the driver will receive.

Be a Driver

CarPoGo makes it easy for users to register as a driver. You must have all the necessary information ready, and our team will inspect the validity of the information. You can register as a driver instantly, since security measures are inplace during the registration process. As a driver you can either post your scheduled ride for later in the day or week. Passengers can view these rides and ask to join. as a driver you can also view passenger rides and ask to be their driver. Since CarPoGo has centralised Hubs, you can have ease of mind knowing the chances of you not knowing where to pick up your passengers, are low. The Hubs also ensure that you can park at a save spot to pick up the passengers. You might already be passing by these Hubs, so why not save on petrol money and make some money while following your usual daily routine.

Our Hubs

The Hubs are carefully selected, conveniently located public locations in the Stellenbosch, Somerset West, Gordons Bay, Strand, Durbanville and Cape Town areas. CarPoGo drivers are only allowed to pick up and drop off passengers at the Hubs and no other place. This ensure effective communication regarding pick up and drop off locations. It also ensures that this is done in the safest way. If the driver has to wait a few minutes for a passenger, they can do so in a safe spot, and not park on the side of the road. Users are encourages to fill out the contact us form and let us know to where we must expand our Hub locations. If you feel a Hub location needs to change or move, please let us know. These Hubs are there to make ride sharing more convenient, safe and easy for you.

Your Safety

GarPoGo values the safety of all drivers and passengers. If you have ever felt unsafe at any stage please make sure to contact us and one of our representatives will reach out to you. We strive to improve where safety might be lacking. We encourage you providing us any feedback you may have. We value your feedback and will act upon it. We want to make ride sharing more convenient, safe and easy for you. As a general rule for drivers, do not pick up anyone that has not scheduled a ride. Also, do not pick up anyone at a location that is not a Hub. As a general rule for passengers, make sure it is your driver before you climb into the car.
