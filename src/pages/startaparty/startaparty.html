What is a party?

By starting a party, you become the party leader, and you can invite other members through sharing the party code with them. They enter the party code in the text box on the ‘Join Party’ page. We recommend limiting a party to 3 members since that is the number of seats a driver tends to have available.

Through creating a party you receive a discounted rate and you can ensure you and your friends are on the same ride. The discounted rate is calculated on the number of people in your party. The bigger the party the bigger the discount.

As a party leader, once all the members have joined you can schedule a ride for the party. You will then be in control and responsible for the ride scheduling process. You will also be responsible for paying for all the members, but you will receive the discount rate based on the size of your party.

Every user can only belong to one party at a time. If you want to create a new party or join another party, you first have to disband the current party.
